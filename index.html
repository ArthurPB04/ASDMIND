Mission

Developing technological solutions that facilitate and enhance the treatment of children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), promoting their cognitive, social and emotional development.

F.A.Q

What is autism and how is it diagnosed? ▼

Autism is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects communication, social interaction and behavior. Autism is heterogeneous, meaning there are different types and degrees of autism. That’s why autism is called Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD).

What are the warning signs for autism in childhood?▼

The warning signs for autism can vary according to the age and level of development of the child

What is the treatment for autism?▼

There is no cure for autism, but there are various forms of treatment that can help the child develop their skills and potentialities, improve their quality of life and their social inclusion. ASDMIND works in order to improve and help the treatment of autism
